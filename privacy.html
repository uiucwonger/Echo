Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2025

Echo Uploader (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Echo Uploader does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from users. The app may access health and workout data locally on your device to provide its core features, but this data is not shared with us or any third parties.

Data Usage

  • Local Device Data: All workout and fitness data is processed and stored locally on your device.
  • Third-Party Services: If you choose to connect your Strava account, data necessary for uploading workouts will be transmitted securely to Strava, in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. Workout data is only shared with Strava if you explicitly choose to upload a workout.

Children’s Privacy

Echo Uploader does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data within the app. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
