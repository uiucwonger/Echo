Echo Uploader App Support

Welcome! This page provides help and answers for using Echo Uploader to connect your Rogue Echo Rower workouts to Strava.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I connect my Strava account?
    Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to log in with your Strava account.
  • Having trouble authenticating?
    Make sure you have a stable internet connection and are using the latest version of the app.
  • Why are my activities showing up as a Run in Strava?
    Strava defaults to "Run" when the sport type in your uploaded TCX file isn't recognized. You can manually change the activity type on Strava after upload, or use a companion service like ActivityFix to automatically correct it.
