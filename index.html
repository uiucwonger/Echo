Strava OAuth App Support

Welcome! This is the support page for the Echo Uploader app. It helps users connect Rogue Echo Rower to Strava activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I connect my Strava account?
    Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to log in with Strava.
  • Having trouble authenticating?
    Make sure you have a stable internet connection and the latest version of the app.
  • Why are my activities showing up as a Run in Strava?
    Strava shows activities as a "Run" because Strava does not read the sport type from the app's uploadedTCX files. You can manually change the activity type on Strava after upload, or use a Strava companion app "ActivityFix" to automatically correct it. ActivityFix
